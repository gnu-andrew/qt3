Qt Commercial Editions

The Qt Professional and Enterprise Editions are the commercial versions of Qt.

You may only write commercial, proprietary and non-free software if you have purchased the Professional Edition or the Enterprise Edition. (1) The purchase of a Qt commercial edition includes technical support and upgrades. Qt for Microsoft Windows is only available in the Professional and Enterprise Editions.

Qt is supported on the following platforms:

  • MS/Windows -- 95, 98, NT 4.0, ME, 2000, and XP
  • Unix/X11 -- Linux, Sun Solaris, HP-UX, Compaq Tru64 UNIX, IBM AIX, SGI IRIX and a wide range of others
  • Macintosh -- Mac OS X
  • Qt/Embedded -- Linux platforms with framebuffer support.
